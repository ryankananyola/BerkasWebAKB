Dufan

Dufan, or Dunia Fantasi, is the largest amusement park in Indonesia located in Ancol, North Jakarta. Covering an area of ​​more than 50 hectares, Dufan offers a variety of rides, attractions, and shows suitable for all ages. From thrilling roller coasters to refreshing water rides, Dufan has something for everyone. In addition, the park also has a special zone for young children, interactive play areas, and entertaining shows. With its lively and colorful atmosphere, Dufan is one of Jakarta's favorite tourist destinations for families and friends.

Jatim Park 1

Jatim Park 1 is a recreational park located in Batu City, East Java, Indonesia. This place is known for combining amusement rides, theme parks, and nature education. Visitors can enjoy various attractions such as amusement rides, interactive museums, and mini zoos. Jatim Park 1 also offers education on various subjects, including science and nature, through entertaining and educational exhibitions for visitors of all ages.

Transportation Museum

Transportation Museum or Museum Angkut is located in Batu City, East Java, Indonesia. This museum is unique because it combines transportation collections from various eras, from horse-drawn carriages to modern cars. Visitors can find interesting replicas of world landmarks, such as the Eiffel Tower and the Taj Mahal, and enjoy interactive rides and shows. The Transportation Museum is suitable for all ages and is a popular tourist destination in Indonesia.

Bogor Safari Park

Bogor Safari Park is a safari park located in Cisarua, Bogor, Indonesia. This park is famous for offering a unique experience of seeing wild animals from various parts of the world in semi-natural habitats. Visitors can explore this park by car or safari bus, and interact directly with some visitor-friendly animals in certain areas. In addition, the park also offers various other attractions such as animal shows, amusement rides, and educational conservation areas.

Trans Studio Bandung

Trans Studio Bandung is an indoor amusement park located in Bandung, West Java, Indonesia. Famous as one of the largest amusement parks in Southeast Asia, Trans Studio Bandung offers various exciting attractions and rides, including amusement rides, shows, and interactive events that entertain people of all ages. With its captivating theme and innovative concept, this place has become a popular destination for both local and international tourists.
