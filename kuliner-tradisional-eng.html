Rendang

Rendang is a meat-based dish produced from the low temperature cooking process for a long time with using various spices and coconut milk. The cooking process it took hours until all that was left was pieces dark black flesh and bran.

Gudeg

Gudeg is a typical dish of the Special Region of Yogyakarta made from young jackfruit cooked in coconut milk. It takes time hours to make a gudeg dish. Normal brown color produced by cooking teak leaves together. Gudeg usually eaten with rice and served with thick coconut milk sauce (areh), free-range chicken, eggs, tempeh, tofu and fried chili sauce crackle.

Rawon

Rawon is a typical Indonesian dish originating from Ponorogo, East Java, which is a black meat soup with a mixture A typical spice that uses kluwek. This food has aged more than 1,000 years. Rawon from Ponorogo spreads to all corners East Java and is known as a typical East Javanese dish.

Pempek

Pempek, mpek-mpek, or in Indonesian slang empek-empek, is a typical food from Palembang, South Sumatra. Process It is made from finely ground fish meat, mixed with starch or sago flour, as well as with the addition of the composition of other ingredients such as eggs, garlic ground, seasoning, and salt. Pempek is usually served with a sauce called cuko which has a sour, sweet, and spicy.

Taliwang Chicken

Taliwang Chicken is a typical food originating from Taliwang, West Sumbawa, West Nusa Tenggara. This food is made from chicken meat. The chicken meat served comes from chickens Kampung Muda is grilled and then seasoned with a kind of sauce ingredients include dried red chilies, shallots, garlic, tomatoes, fried shrimp paste, galangal, brown sugar, and salt. This food is usually served with special foods Another Lombok, for example plecing kangkung.
