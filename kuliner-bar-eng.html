Jenja, Bali

Jenja Club Bali is a restaurant, bar, and club combined into one. Located between Legian and Seminyak, about 200 meters from Double Six Beach, Jenja Club is the hottest new spot in Bali for hanging out and partying. With live music, DJ entertainment, delicious drinks, tasty food, and much more, Jenja Club is a place you must visit if you want to have a fun time in Bali with your friends.

Cloud Lounge & Dining, Jakarta

Cloud Lounge is a rooftop restaurant, lounge, and bar located on the 49th floor of The Plaza on Thamrin Street, right in the heart of Jakarta. Cloud Lounge is a rooftop restaurant, lounge, and bar that offers its customers a delightful tapas dining experience, supported by outstanding service, quality wine menu options, imported beer, premium spirits, and refreshing signature cocktails.

Mirror, Bali

Mirror Bali Lounge and Club offers a stylish lounge and party atmosphere on the main Petitenget Street in Seminyak. The overall design of the venue is inspired by Gothic cathedrals, with shimmering mosaic wall decorations, stained glass windows, and towering ceilings. The club also features futuristic laser lighting systems that provide you with one of the coolest and most immersive spaces for drinking, relaxing, and partying.

The Brotherhood, Jakarta

The Brotherhood Jakarta, a famous bar internationally recognized as one of the best bars in the world by Bloomberg. Located in South Jakarta, Indonesia, this place offers an unmatched luxurious and authentic nightlife experience. The Brotherhood brings together the best mixologists and chefs, creating a vibrant atmosphere with state-of-the-art sound systems and captivating lighting. A favorite spot for local and international musicians and DJs, with appearances by famous names like DEWA, Rosa, Judika, and others. Celebrities from various fields also frequently visit The Brotherhood, adding to its allure.

Atlas Beach Fest, Bali

Atlas Beach Fest is one of the most popular beach clubs in Bali located in the Berawa area of Canggu, North Kuta, Badung. It is strategically located along the beach. It is close and easily accessible from Kuta, Seminyak, Jimbaran, Nusa Dua, to Ubud. No wonder it's always a tourist destination choice. Although it's relatively new, its popularity quickly reaches the market. This place has a bar and outdoor area that can accommodate up to 10,000 visitors. You will definitely enjoy the 52 choices of food and beverage outlets.
