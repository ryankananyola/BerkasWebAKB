Mount Bromo

Mount Bromo or in Tengger language spelled "Brama", too called the Tengger Caldera, is an active volcano in East Java - Indonesia. This mountain has a height of 2,329 meters above sea level and is in four areas districts, namely Probolinggo Regency, Pasuruan Regency, Lumajang Regency, and Malang Regency. Mount Bromo is famous as a major tourist attraction in East Java. As an object tourism, Bromo is interesting because of its status as a mountain still active fire. Mount Bromo is included in the area Bromo Tengger Semeru National Park.

Derawan Island

Derawan Island is located in the Derawan Islands, Derawan District, Berau Regency, East Kalimantan. This tourist island object is the Derawan marine park area which is also known as “The Coral Triangle”. The main attraction is the sea panorama and The beach with its white sand is accompanied by the friendliness of the people local Bajau tribe. This island is known for its marine tourism, especially for diving and snorkeling activities.

Kelimutu Lake

Kelimutu Lake is a three-colored lake on the island Flores in East Nusa Tenggara (NTT), precisely in Ende Regency. This lake is the only three-colored lake in Indonesia Indonesia. This beautiful lake will change color. The three colors that generally appear are blue, green, and blackish red. Changes in the color of the water in the lake Being in the Kelimutu National Park area is an attraction the main tourist destination.

Komodo Island

Komodo Island is an island located in the Nusa Islands Southeast, is to the east of Sumbawa Island, which is separated by Sape Strait. Komodo Island is known as the natural habitat of animals komodo dragon. Administratively, this island is a territory West Manggarai Regency, Komodo District, Nusa Province East Southeast, Indonesia. Komodo Island is the very tip west of East Nusa Tenggara Province, borders the Province West Nusa Tenggara.

Pink Beach

Pink Beach is a beach located in Sekaroh village, Jerowaru sub-district, East Lombok Regency. This beach has The original name is Tangsi Beach. This beach is one of seven beaches in the world that have red sand young and one of two beaches in Indonesia that has sand pink. Tourists who come to Pink Beach can do this many activities, especially activities related to water, such as snorkeling, fishing and diving.
