Gudeg Yu Djum, Yogyakarta

Established since 1950, Gudeg Yu Djum has become an icon of Yogyakarta's culinary scene. Its distinctive taste comes from carefully selected and high-quality ingredients cooked traditionally based on original recipes that have been preserved to this day. Gudeg Yu Djum Wijilan is located at Jalan Wijilan No.167, Yogyakarta. The location is very strategic and easy to visit as it is located in the city center and is a tourist area of Yogyakarta city, namely the Kraton Jogjakarta and the culinary area of Gudeg Wijilan.

Warung Bu Kris, Surabaya

When strolling around Surabaya city, we can visit Warung Bu Kris to add it to our culinary list. Warung Bu Kris itself was established in 1993 and is located at Jl. Kayoon No.46B, Embong Kaliasin, Kec. Genteng, Surabaya, East Java.

Raja Sate, Solo

Raja Sate Solo was established in 1990 and is located at Jl. Honggowongso No.36, Sriwedari, Laweyan, Surakarta City. The owner of this restaurant is Sumanto. This restaurant is famous for its goat and lamb satay with its own distinctive characteristics.

Bebek Bengil, Bali

Bebek Bengil serves authentic Balinese 'crispy duck since 1990', as well as duck satay and smoked duck (which must be ordered a day in advance). The menu here also includes a variety of international and other Indonesian options. The restaurant is part of the Agung Raka group located behind Agung Raka Bungalows and Agung Raka Art Gallery. The first restaurant is located in the middle of Jalan Hanoman, in the Padang Tegal village in Ubud. Just before the turn towards the famous Ubud Monkey Forest Sanctuary. You can find another branch on Monkey Forest Road.

Teras Rumah Nenek, Jakarta

In the southern part of East Jakarta, there is a cozy café worth visiting. In fact, this café has a comfortable atmosphere just like grandma's house. The café is called Teras Rumah Nenek. The concept of togetherness, atmosphere, and hospitality at Teras Rumah Nenek café is the attraction of this hangout spot. Some of its dishes also remind visitors of homemade dishes like grandma used to make. This cool and comfortable place is located in the Cibubur area, East Jakarta. Precisely at Jalan Abdulrahman No.46, Cibubur, Ciracas District, East Jakarta.
