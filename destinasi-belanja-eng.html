Beringharjo Market

Beringharjo Market is located in the center of Yogyakarta City, Indonesia, right next to the south of the Yogyakarta Palace. This market is one of the largest traditional markets in Yogyakarta and offers a variety of merchandise such as batik, fabric, handicrafts, souvenirs, spices, and much more. In addition to shopping, visitors can also experience the unique atmosphere of Indonesian traditional markets and negotiate prices with the traders. Beringharjo Market is also an interesting cultural tourist destination for both local and international tourists.

Gede Market

Gede Market is a traditional market located in the center of Solo City, Central Java, Indonesia. Famous as one of the largest and oldest markets in this city, Gede Market offers a variety of merchandise ranging from clothing, food, agricultural products, local crafts, to antiques. Its main attraction is the bustling, colorful market atmosphere, and the diversity of products offered. This market is also an interesting place to explore the cultural and culinary richness of Java.

Plaza Indonesia

Plaza Indonesia is a luxury shopping center located in the center of Jakarta, Indonesia. Known for offering an exclusive shopping experience with various leading international brands and luxury restaurants. Plaza Indonesia also offers art galleries, cinemas, and five-star hotels, adding to its appeal as a prestigious tourist and shopping destination in Jakarta.

Tanah Abang

Tanah Abang is an area in Jakarta, Indonesia, famous for its bustling wholesale market and variety. Its strategic location, close to the business center, and good transportation access. Tanah Abang Market is one of the largest textile markets in Southeast Asia, offering a wide range of textile products ranging from fabrics, clothing, accessories, to fashion supplies at wholesale prices. The diversity of products and competitive prices make Tanah Abang a favorite shopping destination for entrepreneurs and retail buyers.

Tunjungan Plaza

Tunjungan Plaza is a shopping center located in the heart of Surabaya City, Indonesia. Known as one of the main shopping destinations in the city, Tunjungan Plaza offers various shops, restaurants, and entertainment centers. Known for its grand architectural design and modern facilities, Tunjungan Plaza is a favorite for visitors who want to shop, dine, or simply enjoy the city's atmosphere.
