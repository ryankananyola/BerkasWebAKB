COMO Uma Canggu
Bali

COMO Uma Canggu is situated on the edge of the sand, just steps away from the breaking waves on one of Bali's best surf beaches. As with all our Uma resorts, you can enjoy adventure-filled days, including surf lessons for all levels, cultural and nature excursions, as well as kids' activities at Play by COMO.

Morabito Art Cliff
Bali

Designed by French-based architect and interior designer Pascal Morabito, MORABITO ART CLIFF is a perfect contemporary artwork, offering you the privilege of traveling in the same place in 5 different atmospheres and mystical destinations with Bali's weather and goodness.

Double-Six Luxury Hotel
Bali

Welcome to Double-Six Luxury Hotel, Seminyak, where luxury meets Balinese hospitality. Enjoy an unforgettable experience with captivating ocean views, luxurious facilities, and top-notch service. Make your journey an everlasting memory with us.

Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve
Bali

Located in the heart of Bali's cultural and spiritual Ubud, Mandapa, A Ritz-Carlton Reserve is the third property in the prestigious Ritz-Carlton Reserve collection. An intimate hotel consisting of only 60 villas and suites equipped with two swimming pools, a luxurious spa, and active excursions to awaken the spirit.

Samabe Bali Suites & Villas
Bali

Samabe Bali Suites & Villas features 42 luxury villas, most with ocean views. Its architectural and interior design reflects a harmony that combines contemporary and classic styles that can be traced in the harmonious blend of luxurious and fashionable furniture in the living and bedroom spaces, as well as tiny details in the stylish bathrooms. Garden Pool Villas, Ocean View Pool Villas, and Penthouse Ocean View Pool Villas feature separate living rooms and large terraces with sunbathing areas to allow you to bask under Bali's luxurious sun in deluxe sun loungers, just inches from your private pool.
