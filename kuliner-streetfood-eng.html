Kerak Telur, Jakarta

kerak telur is a food native to the Jakarta (Betawi) area, with Ingredients white sticky rice, chicken or duck eggs, ebi (shrimp marinated dried), added fried shallots, then topped with ground spices in the form of roasted coconut, red chilies, galangal, ginger, turmeric, fine lemongrass, granulated pepper, salt and sugar sand. kerak telur can be found on normal days. You can found kerak telur around Kota Tua, West Jakarta. According to history, Kerak Telor has existed since colonial times In the Netherlands, kerak telur was created by the Betawi people accidentally In the 1920s. This food is quite popular with there are many Betawi people.

Cilok, Bandung

Cilok, the acronym for aci dicolok "skewered tapioca" is a snack made from tapioca, the shape is round and resembles meatballs, filled with lard, meat, and so on, it is usually served with sauce, soy sauce, peanut sauce, and so on. The shape round and sometimes filled with fillings, such as meat or pieces of egg in it. Until now still not It's unknown where cilok actually comes from, but there are many says cilok comes from the city of Bandung.

Bakwan Kawi, Malang

Bakwan kawi Malang is one of the creations from the meatball menu Poor. Usually, in one bowl of bakwan kawi, we will found meatballs, boiled dumplings filled with meat, fried dumplings, and whole tofu doused in broth and topped with sliced ​​leaves onion. Add enough chili sauce and your kawi bakwan eating will be more perfect. Then I often get meatballs too created with various kinds of fillings such as cheese, chili, minced meat, quail eggs, and other additional condiments such as intestines, “tetelan”, and lontong.

Serabi, Solo

Srabi Solo or Serabi Solo is a snack comes from Solo, Central Java. This food is made from flour rice mixed with coconut milk and fried over charcoal similar to pancakes. Srabi is a typical snack made from Solo of coconut milk mixed with a little flour, which makes it taste delicious. Usually also given sprinkles in the form of pieces of banana, jackfruit or even sprinkles and cheese Like. The famous Srabi comes from the Notokusuman area, which often shortened to Notosuman (Srabi Notosuman).

Wedang Ronde, Jogja

Wedang ronde is a type of drink from Indonesia. This drink is a type of traditional herbal drink. Wedang Round is made as a hot drink and falls into the beverage category heat that has content. The main characteristics are shape balls made from nuts and rice flour in water hot made from ginger.
