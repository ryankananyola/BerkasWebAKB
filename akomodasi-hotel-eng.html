Alila Manggis
Bali

Inspired by the early European settlers in the 1800s, renowned architect Bill Bensley conceptualized Capella Ubud, Bali as a tribute to their adventurous spirit. Located amidst dramatic terraced landscapes and nestled among lush rainforests, rice paddies, and the sacred Wos River in the original Balinese artist village of Keliki, this luxury tented camp is a secluded sanctuary that truly captures the imagination of those craving unique experiences, allowing them to enjoy the untouched essence of Ubud's surroundings in private and stylish comfort.

Nihi Sumba
Sumba

As the only resort on the largely undeveloped island of Sumba, NIHI Sumba is situated on 560 hectares of pristine land, including a beautiful and wild private beach stretching over one and a half miles. Initially built as a collection of rustic bungalows for surfers drawn to the globally acclaimed exclusive waves.

Mandapa, a Ritz-Carlton Reserve
Bali

Located in the heart of Bali's cultural and spiritual hub of Ubud, Mandapa, A Ritz-Carlton Reserve is the third property in the prestigious Ritz-Carlton Reserve collection. An intimate hotel comprising only 60 villas and suites complete with two swimming pools, a luxurious spa, and active excursions to ignite the spirit.

Alila Villas Uluwatu
Bali

Uluwatu, Bali Located on one of the highest cliffs in Indonesia, Alila Villas Uluwatu infuses the essence of Bali into every aspect of the property. A stunning destination for tranquility and seclusion—the peace of pristine ocean views and the inner silence that comes with the luxury of space—inspires introspection and discovery.
